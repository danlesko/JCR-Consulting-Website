Biomedical & Biomechanics

  • Performed redesign and analysis of a titanium intramedullary implant to a Polyetheretherketone (PEEK)/Glass fiber composite to better match the properties of bone, and prevent premature loosening of the implant.

  • Designed and developed a continuous fiber/particulate composite total surface hip replacement that would result in longer life, hence lower cost, than an equivalent steel hip joint.

  • Created and won proposals to develop a business area in impact biomechanics to prevent injury to soldiers, which was transitioned to a multi-M $ program.

  • Principal Investigator & Program Manager on efforts to produce physical and computational models of the human for blast and ballistic impact studies.
